with 的用法:
    1. 跟．．．一起
        He lives with his mother. 
        他跟他的媽媽住在一起。
        He goes to school with his sister. 
        他跟他的姊姊一起上學。
    2. 擁有
        Tony is a boy with bright eyes.
        東尼是一位擁有一雙明亮眼睛的男孩。
        Abbie is a girl with a long hair.
        艾比是一位擁有一頭長髮的女孩。
        I read a book with a red cover.
        我讀了一本紅色封面的書。
        I see a bird with big wings.
        我看到一隻長著大翅膀的鳥。
    3. 用
        He draws with a pencil.
        他用鉛筆畫畫。
        He drinks juice with a big cup.
        他用大杯子喝果汁。

時間段的用法:
    1. [in the morning] {在早上}
        I eat breakfast in the morning.
        我早上吃早餐。
    2. [at noon]    {在中午}
        I have lunch at noon.
        我中午吃午餐。
    3. [in the afternoon]   {在下午}
        I play basketball in the afternoon.
        我下午打籃球。
    4. [in the evening] {在傍晚}
        I have dinner in the evening.
        我傍晚吃晚餐。
    5. [at night]   {在晚上}
        I go to bed at night.
        我晚上睡覺。
